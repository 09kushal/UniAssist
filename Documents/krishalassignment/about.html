  • About Goldstar Shoes

    • Goldstar Shoes is one of Nepal’s most iconic and trusted footwear brands, manufactured by Kiran Shoes Manufacturers Pvt. Ltd (KSM). Established in the 1970s, Goldstar has earned a reputation for producing durable, comfortable, and affordable shoes that cater to people of all ages. From students to workers, its footwear is designed to withstand daily wear, school, work, sports, and casual activities.

  • History and Legacy

    • Goldstar began as a mid-priced, value-for-money shoe brand and quickly became popular for its strong build, simple design, and long-lasting quality. Over the years, it expanded its product range from classic PVC and PU shoes to modern sneakers, running shoes, trekking shoes, and school footwear. The Classic G10 model, in particular, is famous for its timeless design and durability, making it one of the best-selling shoes in Nepal.

  • Manufacturing and Production

    • Goldstar operates multiple manufacturing units in Nepal, employing thousands of workers and producing thousands of shoes every day. The company also owns the HATHI brand for slippers and sandals. Many raw materials, including EVA, PVC, and synthetic components, are imported from India, China, and Taiwan to ensure high-quality production.

  • Product Range

    • Goldstar offers a wide variety of footwear:

    1. Classic Shoes: Simple, durable designs for everyday use.
    2. Sneakers: Stylish and comfortable for casual outings.
    3. Running and Sports Shoes: Lightweight and supportive for physical activities.
    4. School Shoes: Designed for comfort during long school hours.
    5. Sandals and Slippers: Under the HATHI brand, suitable for home and casual wear.

  • Market Presence and Exports

    • Goldstar is widely available across Nepal, with many showrooms and retailers in major cities. It has also expanded internationally, exporting to countries like India and the USA. Despite some challenges like import regulations and counterfeits, Goldstar continues to maintain its position as a leading footwear brand.

  • Recognition and Social Impact

    • Goldstar has received national awards for best exporting company and taxpayer recognition, reflecting its contribution to Nepal’s economy. The brand also participates in social initiatives, such as providing shoes and slippers to earthquake-affected communities, demonstrating its commitment to social responsibility.

  • Brand Values

    • Goldstar emphasizes quality, affordability, and reliability, making it a symbol of Nepali craftsmanship. Its products combine comfort, durability, and style, appealing to a broad customer base. Over decades, Goldstar has not only provided shoes but also built a sense of pride in locally made Nepali products, making it a household name and a trusted choice for generations.
